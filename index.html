What Happens Next

1

Review the content

Work through each Word document. Where you see 🟧 CLIENT REVIEW REQUIRED or [CLIENT TO CONFIRM], add your answers directly into the document.

2

Confirm or amend

Send back the reviewed documents with your changes. Anything marked clearly as needing your input is a placeholder — everything else is ready to go.

3

Design begins

Once content is confirmed, the designer works from the approved copy to build each page — no redesigns needed as content changes, because content leads.

4

Build and launch

Pages are built to the approved design with confirmed copy already in place. Final review, sign-off, and go live.
