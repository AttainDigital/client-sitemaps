What Happens Next

1

Confirm the sitemap

Review the visual sitemap and confirm every page that will be needed for the website. Add, remove or rename pages before content is finalised. See the Tasks page for the full sitemap checklist.

2

Review the content

Work through each Word document. Where you see 🟧 CLIENT REVIEW REQUIRED or [CLIENT TO CONFIRM], note your answers ready to add in Asana.

3

Add your notes in Asana

For every amendment, answer, or question — add it as a comment or task update in the Asana project board. This is how we'll receive your feedback.

4

Update the page status in Asana

As you work through each page, update its status in Asana — Draft Written → In Review → Approved. We monitor your progress in real time and will action updates as they come in.

5

Design begins

Once all pages are approved, the designer builds each page from your confirmed copy. Content-first means no redesigns as copy changes.
